## Welcome to Forty One

Forty One is a modern project management platform designed for high-performing teams. We help you streamline your workflows, track issues with precision, and collaborate seamlessly to build outstanding products.

![Forty One platform interface](/intro.png)

This guide will help you get started with Forty One and show you how to make the most of its powerful features.

## What is Forty One?

Forty One provides an integrated suite of tools to manage your product development lifecycle:

- **Plan with Clarity:** Define your goals, map out projects, and organize work into manageable sprints or cycles.
- **Track Progress:** Use our flexible issue tracking to capture tasks, bugs, and features. Monitor progress in real-time with customizable boards and lists.
- **Collaborate Effectively:** Keep your team aligned with shared views, discussions, and notifications.
- **Improve Continuously:** Leverage insights from your work to refine your processes and boost team velocity.

Forty One is built for speed and efficiency, allowing your team to focus on shipping great work, not fighting their tools.
