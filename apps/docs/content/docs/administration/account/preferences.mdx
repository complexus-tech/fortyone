## User Preferences

Personalize your Complexus environment to suit your workflow and visual comfort. Changes made here typically only affect your own user account.

### Appearance

- **Theme**: Choose between light mode, dark mode, or an option to sync with your operating system's theme settings.
  - _Dark Mode_: Reduces eye strain in low-light environments.
  - _Light Mode_: Offers a traditional bright interface.
  - _System Sync_: Automatically matches your OS theme.

### Accessibility

- **Reduced Motion**: Option to minimize animations and transitions for users sensitive to motion.
- **Keyboard Navigation Enhancements**: Settings to improve focus visibility or keyboard interaction cues.

### Regional Settings (If Applicable)

- **Language**: If Complexus supports multiple languages, you might be able to select your preferred language here.
- **Date & Time Formats**: Customize how dates and times are displayed.

### How to Update Your Preferences

1.  Navigate to **Settings** (usually via your user icon or a menu).
2.  Select **Account** from the settings navigation.
3.  Click on **Preferences**.
4.  Adjust the available settings to your liking.
5.  Changes are often applied immediately, or you may need to click a "Save Preferences" button.

_(This page describes common preference options. The actual settings available in Complexus may differ. Refer to your Complexus interface for the specific options available.)_
