## Subscription Management

This section is where workspace administrators can manage the subscription plan for the Complexus workspace, handle billing details, and access payment history.

### Current Plan

- **Plan Details**: View the name of your current subscription plan (e.g., Free, Starter, Pro, Enterprise).
- **Features & Limits**: See a summary of the features and usage limits (e.g., number of users, storage, advanced features) included in your current plan.
- **Renewal Date / Billing Cycle**: Information on when your current subscription renews (e.g., monthly, annually).

### Upgrade or Change Plan

- **Available Plans**: Browse other available subscription plans and compare their features and pricing.
- **Upgrade/Downgrade**: Options to switch to a different plan. This might involve pro-rated charges or credits.

### Payment Method

- **Current Payment Method**: View the payment method on file (e.g., a specific credit card).
- **Update Payment Method**: Add a new credit card or update existing card details.
- **Billing Address**: Manage the billing address associated with your payment method.

### Billing History & Invoices

- **Invoice List**: Access a list of past invoices and payment receipts.
- **Download Invoices**: Typically, you can download PDF versions of your invoices for your records.
  _As referenced in the FAQ, invoices are found on the "Billing page" under a "Recent invoices" section._

### How to Manage Billing & Plans

1.  Navigate to **Settings**.
2.  Select **Workspace** from the settings navigation.
3.  Click on **Billing & plans**.
4.  Review your current plan details, update payment methods, or access invoices as needed.

_(Specific options and the layout may vary depending on the Complexus billing system and your current subscription.)_
