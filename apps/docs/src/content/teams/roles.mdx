# Team Roles and Permissions

Learn about the different roles users can have within teams and what permissions each role grants.

## Available Team Roles

- **Team Lead**: Manages the team, its settings, and has full access to all team features
- **Team Member**: Can create and manage stories, participate in sprints, and collaborate
- **Observer**: Has read-only access to the team's work
- **Guest**: Has limited access to specific stories or features

## Role Permissions

| Permission         | Team Lead | Team Member | Observer | Guest |
| ------------------ | --------- | ----------- | -------- | ----- |
| Create stories     | ✅        | ✅          | ❌       | ❌    |
| Edit stories       | ✅        | ✅          | ❌       | ❌    |
| Move stories       | ✅        | ✅          | ❌       | ❌    |
| Create sprints     | ✅        | ❌          | ❌       | ❌    |
| Add team members   | ✅        | ❌          | ❌       | ❌    |
| View dashboards    | ✅        | ✅          | ✅       | ✅    |
| Configure settings | ✅        | ❌          | ❌       | ❌    |

## Changing Roles

1. Navigate to team settings
2. Open the "Members" tab
3. Find the member whose role you want to change
4. Select a new role from the dropdown
5. Confirm the change
