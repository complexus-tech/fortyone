# User Settings

Learn how to customize your personal workspace and preferences.

## Profile Settings

Manage your personal information:

1. Navigate to Settings > Profile
2. Update your name, email, and contact details
3. Change your profile picture
4. Configure your role and expertise
5. Set your time zone and working hours

## Notification Preferences

Control how and when you receive notifications:

- **Email notifications**: Choose which actions trigger emails
- **In-app notifications**: Configure desktop and browser notifications
- **Digest settings**: Opt for daily or weekly summary emails
- **Mute options**: Temporarily disable specific notification types

## Theme and Display

Customize the visual experience:

1. Choose between light and dark themes
2. Set your default dashboard view
3. Configure story card density and information display
4. Adjust text size and contrast settings
5. Set your default language
