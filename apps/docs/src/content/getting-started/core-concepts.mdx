# Core Concepts

Understanding these fundamental concepts will help you make the most of the project management system.

## Stories

The basic unit of work in the system. Stories represent tasks, features, or requirements that need to be completed.

## Sprints

Time-boxed periods in which specific work is completed. Sprints help teams focus on delivering a set of stories within a defined timeframe.

## Teams

Groups of users working together on projects. Teams can be organized by function, project, or any other structure that suits your organization.

## Objectives

Specific, measurable goals that teams aim to achieve. Objectives help align work with strategic priorities.

## Roadmaps

Visual representations of work planned over time. Roadmaps help teams communicate their plans and track progress toward larger goals.
