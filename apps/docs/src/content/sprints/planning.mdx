# Sprint Planning

Learn how to plan effective sprints that set your team up for success.

## Creating a Sprint

1. Navigate to your team's Sprint board
2. Click "Create Sprint"
3. Set sprint details:
   - Name: A descriptive title for the sprint
   - Duration: Typically 1-4 weeks
   - Start and end dates
   - Sprint goal: What the team aims to achieve
4. Click "Create" to save the sprint

## Selecting Stories for a Sprint

1. Open the sprint planning view
2. Drag stories from the backlog into the sprint
3. Consider team capacity and story point estimates
4. Prioritize stories based on business value and dependencies
5. Confirm the sprint plan with the team

## Setting Sprint Goals

Effective sprint goals:

- Are specific and measurable
- Align with broader objectives
- Provide focus for the team
- Can be completed within the sprint timeframe
